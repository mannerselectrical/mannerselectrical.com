Manners Electrical is a sole trader electrician service based in the heart of Norfolk, providing reliable and efficient electrical work across the county.

Situated within a mile of Norwich city centre, Manners Electrical is ideally positioned to travel across Norfolk.

Standard rates apply within a 15-mile radius of Norwich city centre. For locations beyond this, travel costs will be discussed and agreed upon beforehand to ensure transparency and fairness.

About me

I’m Lewis Manners, a fully qualified JIB approved electrician with 17 years of experience in domestic, commercial, and industrial electrical work. I provide high-quality electrical services across Norfolk, with Public & Products Liability insurance covering up to £5,000,000.

Over the years, I've worked in the UK, Australia and New Zealand, assigned to jobs in hospitals, supermarkets, offices, factories, military bases, warehouses, banks, and residential properties.
